Our Story

Three Decades of Precision.

Founded in 1995, Arihantaa Powertech began as a vision to revolutionize the electrical engineering landscape. For nearly 30 years, we have scaled complex challenges, delivering over 500 projects that form the backbone of modern infrastructure.

Our journey from a local consultancy to a global engineering authority is built on the unwavering principles of technical integrity and intellectual authority. We don't just build systems; we engineer legacies that sustain communities.
